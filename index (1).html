A little about me

Where I'm coming from

I'm currently a sophomore at Northeastern University pursuing a dual degree in Business Administration (with a concentration in Finance & Fintech) and Data Science. I love that my two majors push me to think analytically and creatively at the same time, whether that's building a data pipeline or developing a business strategy.

Outside the classroom, I work as a Teaching Assistant for Discrete Structures, helping students navigate the foundational logic and math that underpins computer science. It's one of my favorite parts of the week because there's something really satisfying about the moment a concept finally clicks for someone.

I'm multilingual, speaking English natively and Spanish and Chinese conversationally, and I love the way language gives you a different lens on the world. I'm also passionate about community: I've volunteered at a local farm supporting food pantries, worked as a poll worker, and am always looking for ways to give back.
